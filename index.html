Quick Memory Rules:

  • ASTC Rule: All (Q1), Sine (Q2), Tangent (Q3), Cosine (Q4).
  • 90° & 270° (y-axis): Functions change to their co-functions (sin ↔ cos, tan ↔ 1/tan).
  • 180° & 360° (x-axis): Functions stay the same (sin → sin, cos → cos).
  • Negative angles (-θ): Equivalent to Quadrant 4 (Cosine is positive, others negative).
